well here you go!!!

Well then welcome to my life and my story. I was born in Portland Oregon and was bumped up and down and all around, traveling all over the west-coast in my younger years. I’m talking about Alaska to California with stops in the states that lay in-between, but mostly my youthful years where spent in the wonderful and beautiful state of Oregon. Therefore, during the school year I was encased by the concrete jungle and living among the hustle a bustle of the ever evolving city life. While my summers were spent in the foot hills with my grandparents enjoying the rolling country side riding dirt-bikes on the family farm, fishing and camping or just splashing around in the local swimming hole.

Thus, this is the time period were I found my love for art and what I now call the design process. In where unlocking what now everyone gets to be a witness too, since childhood. Although, it wasn't all on my own I had the loving hands of an amazing family who were always there and able to give me those little pushes that life sometime require. You all know, the ones that get you over those little humps that you just cant seem to get over. Therefore, I am always in debt to them for keeping the inspiration alive in me and allowing me to always be able to take that next step, no matter which way that step may take me.

Some of these steps have benefited me while others have hindered my abilities. Although, the hindering was all my doing, but we’ll talk about that later. I first would like to focus on the achievements in my life. I have wore meany hats in my journey through life. All of them playing off of my strong strengths as being a creative mind, starting with my first job in the family business. Here is where I was able to acquirer the tools to be a successful artist, team player and the ability to manage that team, while also learning the day to day operations compiled with the in’s and out’s of running a successful business.

Hence, with those soft and hard skills learned in my youth through the job ethics instilled by the family Business, allowed me to go on and be a college graduate. In where I went to the local community college in Central Oregon and prevailed in my abilities and obtained an Associates of Applied Science degree, in the manufacturing technologies industry(mfg.).

Also during my enrollment at the infamous Central Oregon Community College I as well acquire certified accreditation in the fields of quality assurance, manual machining, CNC programming and welding.

These accomplishments so far in life have allowed me to embark on an independent life style, and have giving me the ability to dominate not just the work force, but whatever life has thrown at me. Therefore, while talking about stuff being thrown around and a person. I believe this to be the best place to discuss some of the more harder time and diffidently not the best moments in my life. Some people must travel through the valleys of hell to hopefully one day clawing your way to the mountain tops. Although I feel mine where more like the creators of hell and those mountains, well they where like crawling up an erupted volcano.

My eruption started with a drug addiction in where it created a full on system failure in my life, not once, but twice in my existence. Let me tell you that you can never turn your back on an any addictions you may have in your life. Cause, believe you me it'll try to creep its way right back in when your not paying attention, boy howdy. These system crashes are not, let me repeat are not the high-light of my life. They lead to incarceration not once, but twice in this journey called life.

Although, these where total glitch in the matrix, I didn’t allow these glitches define person. Instead I took the time to dig in deep and reflect on ones self worth. Whereas, by doing so permitted the necessary changes that were required in my life to be a productive individual in society. Where I’ve gone on to be a loving father and husband, while also becoming a very successful tattoo business owner. Where at one point it seemed to be oddly enough that these cards would have never been dealt my way.

Therefore, with that being said yet another shout out to the owner of Dragon Fly Tattoo School. He wasn't going to teach anymore, but seen in me the drive and determination, let alone the creative mind behind it all. So thank you for seeing in me what no one else did and taking that chance on that annoying individual. Whereas, allowing me to take my creativity to the next level and letting Twisted Ink to be born...

Now low and behold, where once stood a staving artist, now blossomed a thriving design tattoo artist. Hence, with that I present a glimpse into that creative world!

That there was my first run at recovery and as I said before once an addict always an addict. I differently forgot that part because I was tested and I failed miserably. So as you may have guessed by now I was off to learn yet another lesson, because obviously I didn’t learn it the first time. Yet once again I went on the internal search to find where everything went wrong. This time I was determined to never let this happen to me again.
