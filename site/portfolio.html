About Gallary

Welcome to my portfolio

As I have said the majority of my life has been spent in the design field either as a metal fabricator or a tattoo artist. It wasn’t till a hiccup in my life that I was introduced to the marvels of coding world and let me tell you what an experience it was, right of the bat, deep left field and out of the ball park I flew. I mean learning HTML5, CSS, flex-box and grid was a blast all these tools at my figure tips and I never knew. I’m telling you what a shame! When it came to JavaScript I had to take a step back alongside with a double take. I mean so much going on local, global, mapping, looping, classes and functions oh my.

Now with all silliness aside I was totally amerced into the matrix and going digital. Then came the front-end library’s and the overwhelming power of jQuery, Bootstrap, Sass and my introduction to React and Redux. This is where my OCD took over, loving the file management with the life-cycle, event-listeners and even set-state capabilities of React. I was fixated with this language and knew if I was going to become a coder this is where I was meant to be. So from this point forward in my coding boot-camp React was my platform, not that you can tell at the moment. Due to a data dump I lost all my projects, but don’t worry as you are reading this I’m solving that problem.

Therefore, with my problem solving capabilities and the new found insight on having buck-ups in place, compiled with the strong understanding of web development has allowed me to become the software developer I am today.
